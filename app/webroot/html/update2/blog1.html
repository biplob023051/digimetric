  • What is Digimetrik?
    Aug 13, 2014 by Mark vincent
    Digimetrik is a knowledge assessment platform for recruiting. Our platform provides field-specific technical tests designed to assess the knowledge level of candidates before and during the hiring process. From Civil Engineering to Electrical Engineering, we provide tests in major engineering fields and subfields. Depending on the level of expertise you are looking for in a candidate, you can select basic, intermediate, or advanced levels tests to accurately measure the knowledge level of the candidate. Digimetrik uses a patent pending Knowledge Adaptive Question and Options (KAQO) technique (i.e. an advanced version of a standardized testing method) to select the best questions to assess the knowledge of a candidate. Our questions are prepared by experts from academia and industry, so they represent the latest knowledge in many technical fields. Standardized tests can be memorized and copied. Our adaptive tests, however, are candidate specific, and the list of questions that a particular candidate encounters is totally different than the list of questions that another candidate applying for the same level position will face. Yet, the overall difficulty of the questions remains the same, so clients can rest assured that candidates are being tested on an equivalent basis.


    Our testing process is simple and consistent. Recruiting for a position using our platform requires minimal effort on behalf of the hiring company. The client only has to create a basic job description and invite candidates to take the test(s) using their email addresses. Candidates will receive notification about the test(s) they need to take. The client will see the performance results immediately after the candidates finish the test(s). Candidates can take the test at anytime and anywhere.


    Clients will able to compare the performance of different candidates through a single interface. Once a company invites candidates to take certain tests, Digimetrik will take care of the rest. Our platform can save significant time and resources during the recruiting process. We will help clients select the right candidate by filtering out individuals with unverified skill sets. This helps our clients avoid having to invite dozens of candidates for an onsite interview. Finally, with our platform, clients will be able to assemble a high quality candidate pool that can always be tapped later in order to select additional candidates for onsite interviews for the same position or recruit candidates for a different position. Whether you are a small business owner looking for technical talent, a medium-sized business trying to hire the best engineers, or a consulting firm looking for consultants with deep knowledge of a specific field, Digimetrik is the ideal platform for assessing the knowledge of candidates in a fast and accurate way.
